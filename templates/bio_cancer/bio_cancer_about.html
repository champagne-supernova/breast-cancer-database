  • Introduction

    • Imaging genomics has emerged as a novel high-throughput method of associating imaging features with genomic data, which has the potential to provide comprehensive intra-tumor, inter-tumor and peri-tumor information non-invasively. High-quality data is essential to the study of imaging genomics. Here, we established a specific database for breast cancer imaging genomics, integrating public data and lab data. It is an easy-used database for user to browser, earch and download both image and genomic data, which providing a comprehensive platform for breast cancer research.
