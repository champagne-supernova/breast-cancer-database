Introduction

Soybean Expression Database (SED) is a repository of gene expression profiles derived entirely from RNA-Seq data analysis on tissues spanning an entire range of soybean growth stages and covering a wide variety of biotic and abiotic treatments.

SED features integration and visualization of gene expression profiles based on curated and quality-controlled RNA-Seq data encompassing diverse tissues and treatments.

SED is a sub-project of Soybean that aims to provide rice reference genome with standardized and accurategene annotations based on a huge volume of omics data and a large number of rice-related literatures.
