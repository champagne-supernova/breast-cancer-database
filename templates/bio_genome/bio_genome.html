Idiogram of the glycine max chromosome

Idiogram from measurements of the glycine max chromosome set, displaying six metacentric and fourteen submetacentric chromosomes.
Note that the chromosomes 1–2; 3–4; 5–6; 9–10; 11–12 and 15–16 show the same mitotic morphology.
Besides, structural changes probably occurred in the chromosomes 13, 14, 17, 18, 19 and 20
and modifications in the arm length ratios of the chromosomes 7 and 8.
					
Mitotic evidence for the tetraploid nature of Glycine max provided by high quality karyograms. Plant Syst Evol
Article · May 2007 · Plant Systematics and Evolution
W. R. Clarindo, …, B. M. G. Alves
